In Part I, Chapter II, the young man encounters a drunken man, Marmeladov, who shares his own story of poverty and despair. Marmeladov's wife, Katerina Ivanovna, is also introduced, and the scene depicts the family's desperate living conditions and the impact of their poverty on their children.
